What usually makes a UPI message suspicious?

PIN to receive money

You should not need to approve a collect request or enter a UPI PIN just to get paid.

QR code for refund

Scanning a QR code can trigger a payment instead of sending money back to you.

Fake buyer confirmation

Scammers often say they already paid and then push a link to "release" or "verify" the transfer.
