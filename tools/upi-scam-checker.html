UPI Scam Checker

Review UPI collect requests, refund links, and QR-code traps before you approve anything.

Use this page for buyer-payment scams, fake refunds, payout links, merchant settlement messages, and requests asking you to enter a UPI PIN to receive money. The main checker preset emphasizes collect-request and payment-release traps.

Common UPI scam red flags
Enter UPI PIN to receive money

You should not need to approve a collect request or enter a PIN just to get paid.

Scan QR to get refund

Scanning a QR code can trigger a payment instead of a refund.

Buyer already paid

Scammers often fake a payment and send a link to "release" the money.

Urgent support call

Pressure to share screens, install apps, or complete support steps quickly is a major warning sign.

What should I add to the checker?

The full chat, collect request wording, refund claim, QR message, and any linked domain or merchant site.

Can it analyze screenshots?

Yes. Open the main tool and upload screenshots of the chat, payment page, or message to run OCR first.

Does it help with non-UPI payment scams too?

Yes. The same tool also covers card-entry traps, phishing pages, fake delivery fees, and marketplace payment scams.
