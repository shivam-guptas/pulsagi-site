FAQ

Quick answers before you rely on the result

Will a clean result mean the message is safe?

No. A lower-risk result means the checker found fewer strong signals, not that the message is guaranteed safe.

What if the domain API is unavailable?

The checker still runs local analysis and falls back to structural domain hints like punycode, many subdomains, or suspicious hostname wording.

Can I share a report with someone else?

Yes. Copy result link creates a compact share URL with summary details rather than the full raw text.

What is the Scam Checker?

The Pulsagi Scam Checker is a free browser-based review tool for suspicious messages, links, fake job offers, and UPI requests. It combines local text analysis with optional OCR and domain reputation checks so you can quickly spot pressure tactics, credential theft attempts, and payment traps without creating an account.

How to use the Scam Checker

  1. Pick a preset or stay on General if the scam type is unclear.
  2. Paste the suspicious content and add optional context like sender, money request, or URL.
  3. Read the score, red flags, and recommended actions before clicking, paying, or replying.

Frequently asked questions

Does it work for screenshots?
Yes. Upload a screenshot and use OCR to pull the text into the analyzer.
Does it store my raw message?
The page works in the browser. Share links only store a compact summary, not the full original message.
Can it check UPI and fake job scams too?
Yes. The same checker supports General, Phishing, Fake Job, and UPI presets.
