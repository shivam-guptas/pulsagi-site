What is the Scam Checker?

The scam checker is a free browser-based review tool for people who want a fast safety check on suspicious messages, links, and payment requests. It helps you identify red flags such as urgency, impersonation, payment pressure, fake jobs, and suspicious UPI or link behavior before you respond. Because the workflow is local and no login required, the tool is useful when you need a quick judgment call on a phone screenshot, chat message, or email excerpt.

How to use the Scam Checker

  1. Paste the suspicious text or upload a screenshot that you want to review.
  2. Read the warning signals, confidence notes, and scam summary generated by the checker.
  3. Use the result to stop engaging, verify the request independently, or share the explanation with someone you are helping.

Frequently asked questions

Can it review different scam types?
Yes. The checker is designed for mixed scam signals across links, fake jobs, payment requests, and impersonation messages.
Should I still verify manually?
Yes. Use the result as a quick risk screen, then confirm details through official channels before taking action.
Why keep it browser based?
A browser-based flow makes it easy to open, test, and share from any device without signing in or installing anything.
