Check suspicious recruiter messages before you pay, reply, or share documents.

Use this page when a recruiter asks for a joining fee, document-verification payment, Telegram interview, or "easy remote work" payout. The main checker preset focuses on no-interview offers, salary promises, impersonation, and advance-fee red flags.

High-risk fake job signals
No interview

Fast hiring with zero screening and unusually high pay is a common scam pattern.

Joining fee

Legitimate employers do not ask candidates to pay to unlock the job.

WhatsApp only

Hiring handled only on WhatsApp or Telegram is a major warning sign.

Document rush

Pressure to send IDs, bank data, or signatures before verification can lead to identity misuse.

What should I paste into the checker?

The full recruiter message, job post text, email domain, fee request, and any payment instructions.

What if I only have a screenshot?

Open the main tool and upload the screenshot to run OCR before analysis.

Can the tool check recruiter domains too?

Yes. If the message includes a domain or link, the report can request backend reputation data.
